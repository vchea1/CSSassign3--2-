About Skittles

Skittles are a fruity, colorful candy known for their vibrant taste and the famous slogan "Taste the Rainbow." Enjoyed worldwide, they bring a burst of flavor in every handful!

Fun Facts

  • Skittles were first introduced in the UK in 1974.
  • The iconic "Taste the Rainbow" slogan is recognized worldwide.
  • They are produced by Mars, Incorporated.
  • Available in a variety of flavors and colors, ensuring a burst of fun in every bite.

Nutritional Information

One serving of Skittles (about 1.5 oz or 42g) typically contains:

  • Calories: 160
  • Total Fat: 0g
  • Sugar: 30g
  • Carbohydrates: 35g
